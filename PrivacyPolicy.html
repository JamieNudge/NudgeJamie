Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 Sep 2025

Nudge is designed to run on‑device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

Data We Access

  • Screen Time/Family Controls: used locally to apply app reminders and shields.
  • Notifications: used to deliver your scheduled reminders.
  • Microphone (optional): only if you record a personal voice cue; audio stays on your device.

Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with third parties. No analytics SDKs are included.

Retention

Settings and reminders are stored on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
